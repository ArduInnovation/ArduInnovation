AirConnect

Powered by Arduino Mega

The general objective of this study is to assess the quality of air by measuring the level of major air pollutants present in the City of Malolos, Bulacan.

    AirConnect, a sample of an air quality index device powered by Arduino microcontroller to measure the levels of air pollutants, also wherein any individual may be able to connect with the device by texting a certain keyword and provide them the status about the air quality within the area.

Specifically, the study will answer the following questions:

 1. What is the level of the five major air pollutants present inside the campus in terms of:

    Particulate matter (PM2.5), Ozone (O3), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Carbon     monoxide     (CO), Sulphur dioxide (SO2), Temperature and Humidity

2. What is the average quality of air in the following areas:

    Malolos Bayan, Tikay, Longos, Diversion (to Paombong) and Brgy. Guinhawa (MacArthur     Highway)
