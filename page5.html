Project AIR

Powered by Arduino Uno

This research aims to develop a low-cost air quality monitor that will monitor the particulate matter inside PUV's and also for the people to be aware what might affect their body and how dangerous the air people breath inside PUV's.

    The researchers’ specific concern and center of attention for producing this kind of invention is global warming and climate change, especially the hazards of air pollution inside public utility vehicles that can severely damage the health of many people in a community.

    This research aims to present an approach to the evaluation of air quality inside a vehicle, especially the causes and effects of harmful microparticles inside PUV’s and it focuses on defining classes in a way which may be useful for improving air quality during a trip. 

    PM 2.5, PM 10, CO2, CO, temperature and humidity are the primary paraeters to be studied in Public Utility Vehicles.
